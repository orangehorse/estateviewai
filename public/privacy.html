Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026 | Last Updated: January 25, 2026

Summary: EstateView AI processes your trust documents entirely in-memory during analysis. We do not store, retain, or have access to your uploaded documents after your browser session ends.

1. Introduction

EstateView AI ("we," "our," or "the Service") is a trust document analysis tool that helps users evaluate their estate planning documents. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and have designed our architecture to minimize data collection and retention.

2. Our Technical Architecture

Understanding how EstateView AI works is essential to understanding our privacy practices. Our Service operates on a minimal-footprint architecture:

📄

Your Browser

Document text extraction happens locally in your browser before any data is transmitted

🖥️

Render.com

Stateless application server that processes requests without persistent storage

🤖

Anthropic Claude API

AI analysis engine that processes document text and returns analysis results

💾

GitHub

Hosts only application source code — no user data is ever stored here

3. Information We Process

3.1 Document Content (Temporary Processing Only)

When you upload a trust document for analysis:

  • Your document is converted to text in your browser
  • The extracted text is transmitted to our server over an encrypted HTTPS connection
  • The text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis
  • Analysis results are returned to your browser
  • No document content is stored on our servers or any persistent database

Key Privacy Protection: Our server is stateless. When your analysis request completes, the document text exists only in your browser. We have no database, no file storage, and no mechanism to retain your documents.

3.2 Technical Data

Like most web services, our hosting provider (Render.com) automatically collects:

  • IP addresses (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Request timestamps
  • General usage metrics

This technical data is managed by Render.com according to their privacy policy and is not combined with any document content.

4. How Your Data Flows

Stage What Happens Data Retention
Upload Document text extracted in your browser Browser memory only
Transmission Text sent via encrypted HTTPS In-transit only
Processing Server forwards to Claude API Server memory during request
Analysis Claude analyzes and responds See Anthropic's policy
Results Analysis returned to your browser Browser memory only
Session End You close browser/tab All data cleared

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Anthropic (Claude API)

Document text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Anthropic's handling of this data is governed by their policies:

  • API inputs are not used to train Claude models
  • Anthropic may retain API logs for safety and abuse monitoring
  • Review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy

5.2 Render.com (Hosting)

Our application is hosted on Render.com. They provide the server infrastructure and may collect technical logs. Review their privacy policy at render.com/privacy

5.3 GitHub (Source Code Only)

Our application source code is hosted on GitHub. No user data is ever transmitted to or stored on GitHub. GitHub hosts only the application code itself.

6. Data Security

Security Measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • No Persistent Storage: We maintain no database of user documents or analysis results
  • Stateless Architecture: Each request is independent; no session data persists on the server
  • No User Accounts: We don't collect emails, passwords, or personal identifiers
  • Client-Side Processing: Document parsing occurs in your browser when possible

7. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not store your uploaded documents
  • We do not retain analysis results after your session
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not sell or share data with advertisers
  • We do not use your documents to train AI models
  • We do not have access to retrieve past analyses

8. Your Responsibilities

Because we do not store your data, you are responsible for:

  • Saving or printing any analysis results you wish to keep
  • Maintaining your own copies of uploaded documents
  • Ensuring you have authority to upload any documents you analyze

Important: Once you close your browser or navigate away, your analysis results cannot be recovered. Download any reports you need before ending your session.

9. Legal Basis for Processing

We process document data based on your explicit consent, provided when you voluntarily upload a document for analysis. You may withdraw this consent at any time by simply not using the Service.

10. Children's Privacy

EstateView AI is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly process information from children.

11. International Users

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, your document text will be transmitted to and processed in the US. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.
